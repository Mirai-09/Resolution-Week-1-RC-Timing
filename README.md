# Resolution-Week-1-RC-Timing
This is a circuit that I designed for Week 1 of Resolution Hardware  
My circuit has LEDs that turn on one by one when the switch is turned on and fade out one by one when my switch is turned off.  
# How it works
It works with the help of RC timing. I have used resistors and capacitors to control the amount of current that goes into the LEDs at a time. The capacitors charge when the switch is on and discharge when the switch is off. As the capcitor that is connect to Voltage charges, it causes a delay in termms of how the LEDs recieve the current, causing them to light up one by one. When the capcitors discharge, they provide current to the LEDs for a bit, which causes them to fade out!<br><br>
